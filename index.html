HOW WE CAN HELP YOU

HF Linux provides on-site and remote consulting services to organizations that rely on a high-performance Linux architecture to achieve their business goals.

Our experience deploying Linux in the financial industry gives us a unique perspective on how to best design, deploy, monitor and optimize your low-latency applications in high-frequency trading environments based on Linux.

Obtaining the best performance out of Linux is not limited to the world finance: we can transpose the same techniques to other fields such as telecommunications and multimedia, where getting the best out of the hardware is often mission-critical.
